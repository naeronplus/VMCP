# E003 — VALIDATION_FAILED

Multi-layer validation failed after staging reimport (worker `run-generation.sh`).

## Layers

| Layer | Source | Failure examples |
|-------|--------|------------------|
| UID integrity | Python scan in `run-generation.sh` | Duplicate `uid://` across `.tscn` / `.tres` / `.gd` |
| Node-path integrity | `workers/scripts/validate_node_paths.gd` (headless Godot `--script`) | Scene fails to load or instantiate under `res://` |

(Optional future / external tooling such as gdtoolkit parse may also map here when integrated.)

## Operator actions

1. Open the job’s validation report (`validation_report.json` under the job S3 staging prefix, or on the runner at `/tmp/staging-$JOB_ID/validation_report.json`).
2. Inspect `layers.uid` / `layers.node_path` and `node_path.errors` / `node_path.logTail`.
3. Review `node_path_validation.log` for Godot `NODE_PATH_ERROR:` lines when the node-path layer failed.
4. Fix scene/resource errors in the generation output, then re-run the job.
